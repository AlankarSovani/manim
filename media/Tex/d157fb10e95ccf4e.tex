Take sequences $a_n$ and $b_n$ such that $a_n \sim b_n$.\\$\lim (b_n - a_n) = \lim (a_n - b_n)$ by properties of limits,\\and since the left-hand side is zero,\\ the right-hand side is also zero.\\Thus, $b_n \sim a_n$.
